Banana Fry


Banana fry is a delicious and crispy dish made by slicing ripe bananas, coating them in a seasoned batter, and deep-frying until golden brown. It is a popular snack in many cultures and can be enjoyed on its own or served with a dipping sauce.


Ingredients:

  • ripe bananas
  • all-purpose flour
  • eggs
  • oil for deep-frying

Steps:

  1. Slice the ripe bananas and set aside.
  2. Mix the all-purpose flour, eggs, and a pinch of salt to create a seasoned batter.
  3. Coat each banana slice in the batter.
  4. Heat the oil in a deep frying pan and fry the battered banana slices until golden brown.
  5. Drain the fried bananas on paper towels and serve hot with a dipping sauce.
