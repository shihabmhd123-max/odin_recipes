Beef Dry Fry


Beef dry fry is a flavorful and spicy dish made by marinating beef strips in a blend of spices and then stir-frying them


Ingredients:

  • beef strips
  • spices
  • oil for stir-frying

Steps:

  1. Marinate the beef strips in a blend of spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large frying pan over high heat
  3. Add the marinated beef strips to the pan and stir-fry until they are cooked through and slightly crispy on the outside.
  4. Serve hot with rice or as a side dish with your favorite curry.
