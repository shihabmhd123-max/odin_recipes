Chicken Biriyani


Chicken Biriyani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with basmati rice, tender chicken pieces, and a blend of spices. It is a popular dish in South Asian cuisine and is often served with raita or a side salad. The chicken is marinated in yogurt and spices.


Ingredients:

  • chicken pieces
  • basmati rice

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt and a blend of spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the basmati rice until it is about 70% cooked and set aside.
  3. In a large pot, layer the marinated chicken and partially cooked rice, starting with a layer of chicken at the bottom and ending with a layer of rice on top.
